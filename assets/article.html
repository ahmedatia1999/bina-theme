Your preparation for the aptitude test can be more effective using a variety of study strategies. Here's an explanation of some of these strategies to help you achieve outstanding academic performance:

Mind map:

Draw a mind map of information visually, start with the main idea and branch out into sub-ideas using colors and graphics. This makes it easier to remember information and clarify the relationships between them.

Active remarks:

Effectively take notes using colors, graphs, and symbols to enhance understanding and remembering.

Divergent repetition:

Review information regularly at intervals. You can use apps like Anki to regulate the repetition process, which enhances long-term memory.

Learning with Education:

Try to teach someone else what you’ve learned or explain the information to yourself out loud. This enhances understanding and remembering by organizing ideas.

Education Cards:

Create educational cards that contain questions and answers and test yourself regularly, helping to quickly and effectively review information.
